Abstract art is a form of artistic expression that does not attempt to depict concrete objects or subjects in the traditional sense. Instead, abstract art focuses on conveying concepts, emotions, ideas, or feelings through the use of shapes, colors, lines, and other visual elements. Abstract art often lacks a clear representation of physical objects or a depiction of reality.
